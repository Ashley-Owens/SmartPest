Frequently Asked Questions


Bedbugs are highly fertile pests that are happy to hitch a ride on human hosts, infested clothing, furniture, or other common items. Some typical causes of home infestation are staying the night in an infested hotel, bringing home an infested item from a second-hand store, or having a guest with bedbugs.


Where there’s one, there’s dozens or even hundreds you can’t see. Pests can destroy property and pose major health risks. Cockroaches, for example, carry numerous bacteria, viruses and even parasites into homes.


At Smart Pest, we don't think “busting out the big guns” is the best approach for most pest situations. It’s unnecessarily toxic, expensive, and disruptive. We like to combine exclusion protocols with tactical precision. In most cases, this means using the least toxic substances first. For example, mesh netting exclusion acts as a physical barrier to keep pesky birds away from your property. We'll provide clear information on what you and your tenants should expect.


For pests like rodents, gophers, squirrels, birds, and raccoons, we follow humane and effective trap-and-release methods whenever possible.


We inspect and treat areas inside the home or building including basements, attics, crawl spaces, hallways, utility rooms, and laundry areas. We also treat exterior structures like sheds, vents, decks, roof ventilation areas, garbage collection areas, and we also treat lawns for pests like fleas and ticks. Anywhere that pests may enter, nest, or feed, we inspect it.
